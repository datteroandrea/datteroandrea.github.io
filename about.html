Birth and Childhood

Birth

I was born in 11/10/2000 at Dolo a small city of Venice, Italy.

I was born with my eyes open, i have always been a curious kid, so i wanted to understand what was happening in this new world as fast as i could...


Childhood

At the age of 6 i started going to elementary school and like every other kid i totally hated that!

I have always been interested in science, but i didn't know what branch i was going to pick, one thing for sure, I LOVED VIDEOGAMES and Computers so i guess my destiny was already written...

As a 2000's kid i loved Dragon Ball and Spider Man and always dreamed about one day becoming a superhero!

I was also A LOT into writing and i wrote many non-released books about superheroes and mystery (mainly inspired by Dragon Ball, Naruto, Spider Man and also Conan The Detective!)

It's time to grow up!

Middle School

Soooo after 5 long years i got into middle school and guess what? I again totally hated that!

Middle school was BAD, i didn't know what i was going to do in the future and had A LOT of normal adolescence problems.

Eventually after 3 long years i graduated middle school and choose the IT path.


High School


Levi Mirano

The first two years of High School i went to Levi High School.

After 2 years at Levi i moved to another school and there everything of my life changed in an irreversible way!


Carlo Zuccante
